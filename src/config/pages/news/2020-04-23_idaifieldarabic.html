Aside from Arabic, the iDAI.tutorial for the use of iDAI.field is also available in German and English. You can enroll in the course by requesting access on the iDAI.tutorial website .
While checking out the course on iDAI.field, you should also take a look at the other tutorials that the DAI offers on data science, information technology and fieldwork and data recording. In the courses you can learn more about using the program QGIS, 3D photogrammetry, SfM (Structure-from-Motion) documentation techniques, as well as many other things.
